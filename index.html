Step 2: Create a wallet on MyEtherWallet

To start, go to myetherwallet.com. Be extra-careful. MyEtherWallet users get often phished through emails that redirect them to website that look like myehterwallet.com but are not myetherwallet.com.
Always control that the name is spelled correctly in the URL and check the SSL. As an extra-safety measure, bookmark the website.


Now enter your strong but easy to remember password and click on “create a new wallet”.
The password only serves to encrypt your private key, the password alone won’t allow you to unlock your wallet.

You can now download the Keystore/UTC file, which is an encrypted version of the private key. Save it on a USB device or in a safe place.

The next screen will show you your private key, save it! You are also given the possibility to print a paper wallet.
Now to access your wallet and be able to send ether and tokens select a method among those offered by myetherwallet.


MyEtherWallet recommends accessing through MetaMask/Mist or through a hardware wallet,
because this way you can access your wallet without having to insert your private key on any website.

Step 3: Buy EOL

As soon as you can see your Ether displayed in your account balance on myetherwallet you are ready to buy EOL.
Type the address of EolCoin’s token sale in the “To Address” field and specify the amount of Ether you intend to send in the designed field.
Remember to send the transaction with at least 200,000 gas. Then click on “generate transaction” to complete the purchase.
